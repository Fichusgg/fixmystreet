Please call us

New and replacement rubbish bins cost:

  • £65 for a 140 litre bin
  • £80 for a 240 litre and above (subject to criteria).

To request one please call our contact centre on 020 8937 5050, Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm (except Bank holidays) or email Recyclemore@brent.gov.uk.

Please note that all requests for new or replacement bins will be assessed by our Waste and Recycling team. If your request is approved by the team, payment must then be received before bins can be delivered.

Current legislation allows the Council to charge you for a general waste bin. You can find out more by reading Section 46 of the Environmental Protection Act 1990.
